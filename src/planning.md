# StudioFlow Planning

# Project Vision
StudioFlow is a web app that helps beauty salon owners and managers organize their daily operations in one place.

----

## Problem
Managing a salon often involves multiple systems and manual processes. StudioFlow aims to simplify daily tasks such as scheduling appointments, managing clients , tracking inventory, and organizing employees.

- i'd like clients' information to be more accesible
- lists of invoices/deposits paid for the day vs. overdue
- 


## Users
- Owner
- Manager
- Employees / Lash Artists
- Receptionist

---

## Pages
- Login
- Dashboard
- Clients
- Appointments / calendar
- Employees
- Inventory
- Settings

---

## Dashboard
Displays:
- Today's date
- Today's appointments
- Upcoming appointments
- Revenue (future)
- Number of clients for the day
- Employees working today
- Low inventory alerts

---
